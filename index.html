Less waiting. Fewer conflicts.

SpinNudge sends automated, transactional (non-marketing) laundry notifications via SMS and, if needed, voice calls to help people pick up laundry on time.

✅ What we send
  • Start confirmations
  • Completion reminders
  • Follow-ups if laundry isn’t retrieved

Message frequency: varies by usage. You may receive multiple messages per laundry session.

📞 Why voice calls?

If SMS is ignored and laundry still isn’t retrieved, we may place a voice call to reduce machine blocking.

🛡️ Consent & opt-out

By manually entering your phone number into the SpinNudge web form and submitting a laundry session request, you provide express consent to receive recurring automated transactional SMS and voice calls related to that session.

  • Reply STOP to opt out of SMS
  • To stop voice calls, contact support@spinnudge.com
  • Reply HELP for help
  • Message & data rates may apply
🚫 No marketing

SpinNudge does not send promotional or marketing messages.
