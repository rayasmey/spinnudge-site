Privacy Policy

Effective date: 02/17/2026

Information we collect

  • Phone number
  • Apartment/room identifier (if provided)
  • Laundry session details (machine ID, cycle type, start time, expected completion time, status)
  • System logs and timestamps necessary to operate the service

How we use information

  • Send SMS notifications related to your laundry session
  • Place automated voice calls when needed (for example, if SMS is ignored and laundry is not retrieved in time)
  • Operate and maintain the service
  • Prevent abuse and ensure system integrity

SMS and voice communications

By entering your phone number and submitting it through SpinNudge, you consent to receive automated SMS and voice calls related to your laundry session.

  • Message frequency varies by usage.
  • Message & data rates may apply.
  • To opt out of SMS, reply STOP. For help, reply HELP.
  • If you no longer wish to receive voice calls, contact us at rayasmey@gmail.com.

Sharing of information

We may share information with service providers necessary to deliver SMS and voice services (e.g., telecommunications providers). We do not sell personal information.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the service and maintain operational records, then securely delete or anonymize it.
