Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 02/17/2026

SpinNudge (“we,” “us,” or “our”) provides automated SMS and voice call notifications related to laundry sessions initiated by users.

Information We Collect

  • Phone number
  • Apartment/room identifier (if provided)
  • Laundry session details (machine ID, cycle type, start time, expected completion time, status)
  • Operational logs and timestamps needed to run and protect the service

How We Use Information

  • Send SMS notifications related to your laundry session
  • Place automated voice calls when needed (for example, if a user fails to respond to SMS notifications or does not retrieve laundry within the expected timeframe)
  • Operate, maintain, and secure the service; prevent abuse

Sharing

We may share information with service providers necessary to deliver SMS/voice services (e.g., telecommunications providers). We do not sell personal information.

Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service and maintain operational records, then deleted or anonymized where appropriate.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of SMS by replying STOP
  • For help, reply HELP
  • If you no longer wish to receive voice calls, contact us using the email below
