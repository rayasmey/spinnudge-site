Privacy Policy

Effective date: 02/17/2026

SpinNudge (“we,” “us,” or “our”) provides automated laundry-session notifications via SMS and voice calls. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Phone number
  • Apartment/room identifier (if provided)
  • Laundry session details (machine ID, cycle type, start time, expected completion time, status)
  • System logs and timestamps necessary to operate and secure the service

How we use information

  • Send SMS notifications related to your laundry session
  • Place automated voice calls if needed (for example, if a user does not respond to SMS or does not retrieve laundry within the expected timeframe)
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the service
  • Prevent abuse and protect system integrity

SMS and voice communications

By entering your phone number and submitting it through SpinNudge, you consent to receive automated SMS and voice calls related to your laundry session. Message frequency varies by usage. Message & data rates may apply.

Sharing of information

We may share information with service providers strictly as needed to deliver SMS and voice services (e.g., telecommunications providers). We do not sell personal information.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the service and maintain operational records, then delete or anonymize it when feasible.

Your choices

  • SMS opt-out: Reply STOP to unsubscribe from SMS messages
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance
  • Voice-call opt-out: Contact us to request removal from voice call notifications
