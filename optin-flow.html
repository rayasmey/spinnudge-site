Opt-In Flow

Public explanation of how users consent to receive SpinNudge transactional SMS notifications.

How users opt in

  1. A user scans a QR code posted on a laundry machine in their residential building.
  2. The QR code opens the SpinNudge session-start page for that specific machine.
  3. The user manually enters their mobile phone number, apartment or room, and cycle selection.
  4. Before starting the session, the page shows the consent disclosure for SpinNudge transactional SMS notifications.
  5. The disclosure states that message frequency varies by laundry session, message and data rates may apply, and users can reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.
  6. The page also links to the Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.
  7. The user opts in only after pressing Continue to start the session.

Consent text shown on the session page

By entering your phone number and starting a session, you agree to receive automated transactional SMS notifications from SpinNudge regarding your laundry cycle, including completion alerts and reminder notifications. Message frequency varies by laundry session. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help. See our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.
