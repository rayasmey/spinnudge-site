Service description

SpinNudge provides automated SMS and voice call notifications related to laundry sessions initiated by users. These notifications may include start confirmations, completion reminders, and escalation messages/calls if laundry is not retrieved within the expected timeframe.

Consent to communications

By entering your phone number and submitting it through SpinNudge, you expressly consent to receive:

  • Automated SMS messages
  • Automated voice calls (including escalation calls if you do not respond to SMS or do not retrieve laundry on time)

Message frequency varies by usage. Message & data rates may apply depending on your carrier.

Opt-out and support

  • SMS opt-out: Reply STOP to unsubscribe from SMS messages
  • Help: Reply HELP for support
  • Voice-call opt-out: Contact us to request removal from voice call notifications

No marketing

SpinNudge does not send promotional or marketing messages.
