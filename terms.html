SpinNudge provides recurring automated, transactional SMS and voice call notifications related to laundry sessions initiated by users.

By entering your phone number and submitting a session, you consent to receive recurring automated, transactional SMS messages and voice calls from SpinNudge related to that laundry session. Consent is not a condition of any purchase.

  • Automated SMS: start confirmations, completion reminders, follow-ups.

  • Message & data rates may apply; voice call charges may apply depending on your carrier/plan.
